Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? Check out our frequently asked questions or reach out if you need more help!

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website can vary depending on the complexity of your project. On average, a simple site takes about 2-4 weeks to complete, while more complex sites may take longer. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What do I need to get started?

To kick off your website project, you'll need to provide details about your business, any content you want to include (like text and images), and your preferences for design and functionality. Don’t worry; we’ll guide you through the process!

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All the websites I create are designed to be responsive, ensuring they look great and function well on all devices, from desktops to smartphones.

What happens if I need support after the website is live?

I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to help you with any issues or updates you may need after your site is launched. You won’t be left alone!

Will my website be optimized for search engines?

Yes! I include SEO optimization in all my website packages to help improve your visibility in search engines and attract more visitors.

Can you create a website just for my business needs?

Yes, I build unique websites specifically designed for your business, making sure they include everything you need to showcase your services and attract customers.
